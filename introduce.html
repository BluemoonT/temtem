The reason I made this site

As I play music games, I often hear that arcade wants to buy games

However, I found out that new game arcades with no relations with old ones have a hard time buying games

They don't know if other game centers want to sell games, and it is too expensive to buy one.

And I also heard that it is troublesome to get rid of game which is no longer needed.

So I made this site to make you feel easy dealing with this problem.
